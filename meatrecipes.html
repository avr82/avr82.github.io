Cuban Ropa Vieja

Yield: 8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of flank steak
  • 3 bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 1 white onion, thinly sliced
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 cup of white wine
  • 1 14-ounces can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 celery
  • 1/2 cup of olives, optional

Directions

  1. Wash and clean the steak. Pat dry and in a hot pan, sear both sides of the flank steak for about 5 minutes.
  2. Once the steak is slightly cooked, in a large pot, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Once the oil is hot, add all your chopped vegetables. Stirring frequently for about 5-7 minutes. Lower the heat to medium-low and deglaze the pan by adding the white wine. Once the alcohol
  3. After cooking the vegetables, add in tomato paste and 14-ounce can of crushed tomatoes, along with all seasonings. Add the chicken broth, 2 bay leaves, and flank steak.
  4. Simmer, uncovered over medium heat until the sauce reduces and thickens. Taste the broth and adjust seasonings to preferred preference. Lower heat to medium-low, covered, and allow to cook for 1 hour.
  5. After cooking for an hour, remove the flank steak and shred. Remove the 2 bay leaves, then add the shredded steak back into the pot.
  6. Serve, and enjoy!
