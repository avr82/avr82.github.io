Some of my favorite hobbies are arts and crafts, trying new resturants, and cooking.

Trying new resturants has become a new hobby and considering how many resturants there are in Miami, it is something that will always keep me busy.

Life as a Vegetarian

Growing up, I was a very picky eater. My diet consisted of ham sandwiches, croquetas (a Cuban dish), and other forms of pork and chicken. When I was 16 years old, I stumbled upon a documentary explaining the negative effects of meat and showed how the animals were being treated. From that day forward, I never touched a piece of meat again.

This pushed me to be creative with the foods I was eating. Since most restaurants at the time lacked meatless options, I would use cooking at home as a way to eat alternative foods. Since it has been over 6 years, I have made tons of recipes I love to share with meat-eaters.
